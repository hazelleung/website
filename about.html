Hi, I'm Hazel.

I'm an aspiring UX designer and researcher with experience in web design, web development, print design, and scientific research. I am a bay area native, but I am currently enjoying the great San Diego weather as I am studying Cognitive Science with a Human-Computer-Interaction specialization the University of California, San Diego.

To me, design is not just a way to make things look nice; it is a way to make information more accessible. Good design means more user friendly. User friendly means a good user experience. A good user experience means they are able to find the information they wanted!
